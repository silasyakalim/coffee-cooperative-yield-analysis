In this analysis, we aim to evaluate the performance of different coffee cooperatives by analyzing key metrics related to coffee production. The primary objectives are to understand how different yield classes contribute to the overall coffee volume and the distribution of farmers across these classes.

**OBJECTIVES** 
Assess Yield Distribution: Determine the distribution of coffee yield across various classes and understand the proportion of farmers and coffee volume within each yield class for different cooperatives.
Analyze Farmer Distribution: Evaluate the percentage of farmers falling into each yield class and how this distribution impacts the overall coffee production.
Measure Volume Contribution: Calculate and compare the contribution of each yield class to the total coffee volume produced by each cooperative.
Identify Performance Trends: Identify and compare trends in performance across cooperatives, highlighting areas of strength and potential improvement.
